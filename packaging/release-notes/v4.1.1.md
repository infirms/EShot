## EShot v4.1.1

This maintenance release fixes capture panel interaction issues on Linux.

### Fixes

- Restored capture keyboard shortcuts after closing the upload or OCR dialog
- Fixed `Ctrl+C` no longer working after returning to the capture panel
- Fixed the annotation color picker freezing under KDE Plasma and GNOME on XWayland
- Made the vertical Quick Settings tab resize for longer translations such as Russian
- Kept the translated Quick Settings tab inside the available screen height
